Sweden

Sweden is a country in Northern Europe, located on the Scandinavian Peninsula. Its capital city is Stockholm. Sweden is known for its high quality of life, beautiful nature, and strong education system. The country is famous for companies like IKEA, Volvo, and Spotify. Sweden is a constitutional monarchy and a member of the European Union.

Info about Sweden

  1. Location: Sweden is in Northern Europe, on the Scandinavian Peninsula.
  2. Capital: The capital city is Stockholm.
  3. Population: About 10 million people live in Sweden.
